Subtitle: Vici a fictitiously futuristic wearable for spatial perception

----

Text: 

Vici is a fictitiously futuristic wearable for spatial perception. The device measures the pulse of the wearer and sends this data to all Vici devices nearby. Vici makes this data audible but the volume varies of the distance between the Vici devices. Vici should give you a new sense of recognizing your human enhanced environment. 
This project was realized in cooperation with: Florian Wachter (IAD), Lorena Strohner (IAD)
